About Us:

Yay for Yoga offers a warm friendly environment where you can either start or continue your Yoga journey. The beauty of Yoga is that it is a personal journey. We want to help people connect with how they feel, how they move and handle challenges with ease.


We are passionate about making Yoga for everyone. We offer a range of Yoga classes seven days a week. Classes are tailor made to suit all levels of Yoga. You will also learn techniques that will help you manage stress and anxiety when you are on the go.


Join the Yay for Yoga team today and feel the amazing transformation for yourself!
